``` text [操作说明]
Welcome to Earth OS!

It can do a lot of things, such as looking in the mirror , listening to music, accessing the Internet... ! It also supports password secrecy OS!

More features
1️⃣ Music:
⭐️ A total of 18+ songs are included, including 9 English songs, 5 Japanese songs, 3 Chinese songs, and a Korean song
▶️ However, due to the size of the project, only 6 songs are included in this version. The remaining 12 songs will be added in the later Earth OS version!
----------------------------------------------------------------------
2️⃣ Change decimals to Fractions:
Click the blue button to input the numbers.

When you input a big decimal in it, it will load very slowly (like if you input 0.142857, it may loading about 2h...), but it won't get stuck. Just wait.
----------------------------------------------------------------------
3️⃣ Logarithm calculator:
Click the [log (In / Ib / Ig) ] button to change the type.
Click the other blue button to input the numbers.

This one use the base changing formula for logarithms.

Logarithm is to calculate the exponent;
Antilogarithm is the answer of power;
"lb" means the base number is 2;
"㏑" means the base number is e;
"lg" means the base number is 10;

e.g. ㏒₂8=lb8=3 (2³=8)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
4️⃣ ☁ Don't get out of the white area!
Control the black ball, avoid various obstacles, and reach the end point, which is the red ball
```
